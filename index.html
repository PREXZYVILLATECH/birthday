Happy Birthday

For someone truly irreplaceable

✦

Today, the universe decided to celebrate

the day you came into this world —

and honestly, how could it not?

Happy Birthday, Nicky. 💙

I've been trying to find the right words

to describe what you mean to me,

and I keep running out of language.

Because some people are simply beyond words.

You are one of those people.

You have this rare and beautiful gift —

you make everyone around you feel seen,

feel heard, feel safe.

In a world that can be loud and unkind,

you are a quiet and steady kind of warmth.

The kind that stays.

Thank you for every conversation,

every word of encouragement you offered

when I needed it most —

even when you had your own storms to weather.

That is who you are, Nicky.

Someone who shows up. Every single time.

Your kindness isn't performative.

It is genuine, deep, and effortless.

You care in ways most people have forgotten how to.

And that is not a small thing.

That is everything.

The distance between us changes nothing.

You are one of the most real friendships I have.

Online or not, near or far,

you have carved a place in my life

that no one else could ever fill.

On this birthday, I want you to feel

every bit of the love you pour into others

poured right back into you.

I want you to rest in knowing

that you are deeply, genuinely treasured —

not for what you do,

but simply for who you are.

May this year bring you everything

your beautiful heart deserves.

Peace. Laughter. Abundance. Joy.

May every good thing find its way to you.

May you receive the same softness

that you so freely give to everyone else.

You deserve the whole sky, Nicky.

Every star in it.

Every shade of blue.

Happy Birthday, my dearest friend. 💙

With all the love in the world —

always and always.
